Paneer Butter Masala


Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy North Indian dish featuring soft paneer cubes in a mildly spiced tomato-based gravy. It’s a crowd-pleaser served with naan or rice.

This simplified version uses everyday ingredients to deliver restaurant-like flavor at home.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 cup cream or milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add onions and sauté until soft.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add garam masala, chili powder, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add paneer cubes and mix gently. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add cream and simmer for a minute. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
