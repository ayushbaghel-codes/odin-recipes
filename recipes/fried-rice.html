Onion Fried Rice


Description

This onion fried rice is a quick, minimal-ingredient dish that's flavorful and filling. Perfect as a standalone meal or as a side with curries or stir-fries.

With just onions, cooked rice, and a few pantry staples, you can whip this up in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Sprinkle turmeric and mix well.
  4. Add the cooked rice and toss until everything is evenly coated and warm.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with coriander and serve.
