Homemade Chowmein


Description

This homemade chowmein is a simple yet flavorful Indo-Chinese dish, packed with vegetables and tossed in a savory sauce. It's quick to make and perfect for lunch or dinner.

Inspired by Indian street-style noodles, this version balances spice, sweetness, and umami with minimal ingredients and maximum taste.

Ingredients

  • 150g noodles (boiled and drained)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • 1/2 cup julienned carrots
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon green chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil noodles until al dente, drain, and toss with a little oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or pan. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté briefly.
  3. Add onions, carrots, and cabbage. Stir-fry on high heat for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar. Mix well.
  5. Add the boiled noodles and toss everything together until well coated.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
